Global Health Insights

Exploring the connections between GDP, national health expenditure, and capital healthcare spending to uncover patterns that shape global well-being.

Our Overarching Story

In a rapidly evolving world, economic disparities significantly impact global health outcomes. Countries with high GDP often allocate less towards healthcare relative to their wealth, while smaller nations prioritize healthcare investments to enhance citizens' well-being.

This website presents a comprehensive analysis of global GDP distribution, national healthcare expenditures, and the stark contrasts in healthcare spending between nations and their capitals. Through visual storytelling, we aim to uncover the hidden patterns that shape global health trends and inspire data-driven decisions for a healthier world.
